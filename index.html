About

Hello! I am a first-year PhD student at Harvard University in the Department of Sociology. My research focuses on the reciprocal relationship between people and the structures under which they live.

My past research projects have focused around culture and cognition, addresssing how people form and update their beliefs about political issues, such as racial wealth gaps and fentanyl overdose.

I am also an aspiring science writer. I am a monthly contributor for Filter magazine, where I cover new studies about harm reduction and substance use across a wide range of academic disciplines. I'm also building a portfolio of essays and op-eds based on insights from social science.
